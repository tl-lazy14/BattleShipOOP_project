
 Welcome to Batteship - a game full of strategy and luck
 Game instructions:

 - Each side has a sea of 10 * 10 coordinates
 - The player will hide 5 ships in turn. From 1 to 5 into their own 
 waters
 - In turn, parties will shoot into the squares of the changing
 waters, if they are hit, they will be fired
 - A ship is down when it is fully shot
 - Game ends when one side is hit by an entire ship
